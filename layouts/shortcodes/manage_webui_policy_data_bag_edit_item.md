To edit a data bag item:

1.  Open the Chef management console.

2.  Click **Policy**.

3.  Click **Data Bags**.

4.  Select a data bag.

5.  Select the **Items** tab.

6.  Select an item.

7.  Click **Edit**.

    ![image](/images/step_manage_webui_policy_data_bag_edit_item.png)

8.  Make your changes.

9.  Click **Save Item**.
